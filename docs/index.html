Why ExpLens?

Multi-dimensional Analysis

Go beyond simple categories. Analyze by purpose, family member, tax deductions, credit card rewards, and unlimited custom dimensions.

Fast, Bidirectional Sync

Download years of transactions in seconds. Categorize in your spreadsheet and sync back to the cloud. Use multiple workbooks sharing the same data.

Rich Banking Metadata

The ExpLens Solution

ExpLens downloads your complete banking data into Excel, enabling truly flexible analysis across unlimited dimensions.

The Problem with Traditional Finance Software

Most apps only categorize by what you bought, leaving critical questions unanswered: What was the purpose of the purchase? Which family member? Small Business deduction?
Other apps do not support Multiple Categories and lack Structure for pivot tables, charting and comprehensive analysis of these dimensions.

Unlimited Categories Made Easy

ExpLens lets you categorize by as many independent dimensions as you want - something typically found only in professional software. It makes multi-dimensional analysis simple and accessible for everyone. Pivots and charts are a breeze.

Automatic Rich Metadata

Transactions come with automatic pre-categorization, merchant details, and credit card points potential - all provided by your banking provider without manual entry.
Auto-categorization rules? Fully supported via Lunch Money, our bank data provider.

Excel Power

Use pivot tables, charts, formulas, and all Excel features you know to create the analysis that matters to you.

Lunch Money Data Access

ExpLens connects to your bank data through Lunch Money, which stores your financial information securely on their servers. Lunch Money requires a subscription:

  • Free trial available to test the service
  • Pay-what-you-want pricing: Starting at $50/year or $10/month - Learn more
  • Long-term sustainable pricing model that respects users

Why two services? ExpLens provides advanced analysis tools, while Lunch Money handles secure bank connections and data storage. This separation keeps ExpLens focused on analysis while ensuring your banking data is managed by specialists.
